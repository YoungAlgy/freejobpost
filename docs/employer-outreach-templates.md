# Employer outreach email templates

**Owner:** Algernon / Ava Health Partners LLC  
**Send pace:** 10–20 emails per week, manually reviewed and sent one-by-one  
**From:** alex@avahealth.co  
**Standard:** S1 (cold-outreach) + S2 (human-touch) — no bulk blast, no automation

---

## Who to target first (Phase 1 priority list)

1. **Mid-market health systems** — 50–500 employees, Florida/Texas/California first (highest job density)
2. **Independent physician groups** — 10–100 physician practices, especially surgical, hospitalist, EM
3. **Behavioral health / substance use** — LCSW/LMHC shortages, often priced out of Indeed
4. **Long-term care / SNF operators** — perpetually short-staffed, heavy Indeed spend
5. **Ambulatory surgery centers** — tight budgets, highly motivated to reduce HR costs
6. **Travel nursing agencies** — high volume posters, feed could drive significant listing count

**Research method:** LinkedIn search `[role] hiring manager [city] site:linkedin.com/in`, Indeed/Glassdoor employer profiles in target markets, state healthcare association member directories.

## Anti-patterns — never do these

- Don't send to personal email addresses found on LinkedIn (use professional contact info only)
- Don't claim statistics ("500 employers use us") that aren't factually true yet
- Don't promise specific applicant counts — we can promise distribution, not conversion
- Don't attach files — plain text emails get better deliverability
- Don't send more than once per 30 days to the same contact
- Don't use "regarding" or other formality tells

## Tracking

Log each send in a simple spreadsheet: Date | First name | Company | Title | Email | Template | Reply (Y/N) | Outcome.  
Re-verify email addresses quarterly — bounce rate above 5% triggers domain deliverability review.
