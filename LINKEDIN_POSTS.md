## How to schedule each one

In your Chrome window with the LinkedIn page open:
1. Click **Page posts** in the left sidebar → **Start a post**
2. Paste the post text (above)
3. Click the **clock icon** (⏰) at the bottom-left of the compose dialog (next to Post)
4. Pick the date + time from the suggested schedule above
5. Click **Schedule**

The post goes out at that exact time — you don't need to be online.

**Post 10 note:** Don't schedule it until you have a real traction number to replace the placeholder with. Posting a generic milestone post before you have one is worse than skipping the slot.
